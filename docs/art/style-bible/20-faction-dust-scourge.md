# Faction Style Bible — Dust Scourge

**Thesis:** the world's scavengers weaponized its poison. Gas, salvage, and survival by strip-mining the dead — a faction that doesn't maintain equipment, it *digests* it. (Mechanical hooks per GDD: gas warfare, sell-refund/salvage economy.)

Deviations from the [game bible](00-style-bible.md) baseline:

## Accent

**Acid yellow-green.** Gas reservoirs, canister windows, mask lenses, chemical sludge, ability glow. The accent is *contained liquid/vapor* — it appears where the poison lives (tanks, tubes, lens glass, vents), so every glint of acid green on a silhouette tells you where the dangerous part is. Max-rank prestige: more visible reservoirs, brighter lens glow — a veteran Scourge unit carries more poison, not more polish.

Legibility constraint (game bible §5.2): generic gas/poison status VFX must stay distinguishable from the faction accent — status gas renders as *billowing translucent cloud*, faction accent as *hard contained glow*. Cloud vs. glass; never confusable.

## Materials & palette

Rag-tan canvas wraps, rust-brown scrap plate, cracked rubber hosing, bone, sun-bleached fabric. Wear pattern: *corroded and improvised* — nothing matches, everything is patched with something else's parts. Where IronMarch is regulation, Scourge is accumulation.

## Silhouette motifs

- **Hunched + asymmetric.** Bent postures under scavenged loads, lopsided armor (one plated shoulder), trailing hoses and straps that break the body's outline.
- **Mask-first faces.** Every unit's head silhouette is a gas mask variant — round filters, proboscis hoses, goggle domes. The mask is the faction's face.
- Vehicles/buildings: welded scrap geometry, visible mismatched patch plates, tank-and-tube clusters. The HQ silhouette is a refinery of stolen parts.

## VFX flavor

Ordnance reads dirty: sputtering flashes, wobbling arcs, lingering vapor trails. Faction abilities bloom in acid green clouds and splashes — Scourge fights leave residue on the battlefield longer than anyone else's (within the ambient-tier budget; it must fade before it stacks into noise).

## Do / Don't

Do: visible jury-rigging, trophies and hooks, drips and stains, silhouette asymmetry. Don't: clean edges, matched kit, brass anything, straight-backed posture. A Scourge unit standing at attention is off-model.
