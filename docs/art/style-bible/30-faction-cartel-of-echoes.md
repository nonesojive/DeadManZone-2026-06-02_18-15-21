# Faction Style Bible — Cartel of Echoes

**Thesis:** the war's ghosts, organized. Stealth, signals, and things heard on dead frequencies — a criminal syndicate that trades in what the zone whispers. Where IronMarch holds ground and Scourge strips it, the Cartel was never officially there. (Mechanical hooks per GDD: stealth/echo synergies.)

Deviations from the [game bible](00-style-bible.md) baseline:

## Accent

**Spectral violet-magenta.** Radio tube glow, antenna tips, lens flare in dark goggles, echo/ability shimmer. The accent is *emission* — it glows from equipment and effects, never painted on cloth. The classic "dark faction" signal (cf. Top Troops' Infernal: desaturated body, glowing accent eyes) — a Cartel unit is mostly shadow with two or three points of violet light. Max-rank: more emission points, never brighter cloth.

Legibility constraint (game bible §5.2): arcane/echo *status* violet must differ from faction accent in placement — statuses sit in the above-head symbol pipeline; the accent lives on the body's equipment. Distinct positions, no confusion.

## Materials & palette

Charcoal oilcloth, black rubberized capes, matte webbing, dark banded metal, wet-slate grey. The narrowest value range of the three factions — Cartel bodies sit closer to silhouette-black than anyone else, and the ink outline does extra work keeping them separated from shadowed ground (side-channel rim + base ring guarantee the floor read; arena spec §3).

## Silhouette motifs

- **Draped + slim.** Long coats, hoods, capes that smooth the body into a single flowing shape — the anti-IronMarch: no hard angles, no visible mechanism.
- **Antenna language.** Whip aerials, back-mounted radio rigs, dish and coil shapes. Where Scourge's silhouette-breakers are hoses, the Cartel's are signal equipment.
- Buildings: lattice masts, wire spans, shuttered listening posts. The HQ silhouette is a black tower dressed in aerials — tall, thin, and quiet next to IronMarch's foundry mass.

## VFX flavor

Understated until it isn't: suppressed muzzle blinks, near-invisible tracers — then echo abilities land as violet ripple/distortion effects (heat-haze rings, afterimage ghosts). Stealth states use the body pipeline (transparency/dissolve on the model); reveals get a punch-in-worthy violet snap.

## Do / Don't

Do: hard shadow shapes, minimal glow points, afterimages, asymmetric drape. Don't: bright cloth, brass or acid colors, bulky armor mass, honest soldiering. A Cartel unit that reads "military" is off-model — it should read "someone you shouldn't have seen."
